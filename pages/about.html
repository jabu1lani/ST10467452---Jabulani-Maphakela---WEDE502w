About Page

GreenEarth Community Garden is a Melbourne-based non-profit organisation established in 2015 with a focus on urban sustainability and community engagement. Through its network of three inner-city gardens, the organisation provides hands-on gardening workshops, volunteer opportunities, and educational programs for all age groups.

The primary mission is “to foster environmental stewardship through practical gardening initiatives,” while the vision centres on “creating greener and more self-sufficient urban communities.”

The target audience includes residents interested in sustainable living, school groups seeking outdoor education experiences, and environmental advocates looking for volunteer opportunities.
